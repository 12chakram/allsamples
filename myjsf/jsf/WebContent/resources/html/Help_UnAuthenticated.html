Frequently Asked Questions
Answers to Frequently Asked Questions
01. How do I create an account?
UltraViolet Accounts can be created at any UltraViolet Retailer or on www.uvvu.com (click "Create Your Free UltraViolet Account" to get started). You must be 18 or older to create an UltraViolet Account. As the first member of your account, you will be given Full Access membership privileges. These allow you to create and manage other members in the account, including setting their Access Levels and Parental Controls. Creating an UltraViolet Account does not require a credit card or any other financial information. You only need to create a unique username and provide an email address that can be confirmed to ensure that we can get in touch with you. In addition, you will be asked to provide the answers to two Secret Questions to help reset your password if you forget it. Finally, you will need to accept the UltraViolet Terms of Use. Once you have created an account and accepted the Terms of Use, you will have access to the UltraViolet Account System where you can add and manage other account members, add and manage apps and devices, and view content rights that you have added to your UltraViolet Digital Rights Locker. After you create an UltraViolet Account, you have 72 hours to confirm the email address that you provided to us by clicking through a link in an email we send to you. If you have not confirmed your email within 72 hours, your member sign in will be locked until you do so. If your member sign in is locked, you will be given specific instructions on how to unlock it when you attempt to sign in to the UltraViolet Account System. While your member sign in is locked, none of the content rights or registered apps & devices in your account will be affected, and you will be able to access them immediately after you confirm your email address.
02. What is an "account name"?
An account name is a friendly name that you give to your UltraViolet Account. It will be used to label some of the pages on the UltraViolet Account System to remind you that you are signed in. An account name can have any combination of up to 50 letters, numbers and characters (including spaces).
03. Are there guidelines for creating my username?
Your username is the unique name that the Ultraviolet system will use to identify you. We will check your username at the time that you create it; if someone has already created a member profile with that name you will be instructed to choose a different name. You can change your username at any time by signing into the UltraViolet Account System, going to the “My Account” section, and clicking on your name or icon. You username must have at least 6 letters or numbers and no spaces. It may include any of the following characters ! . @ # $ % & * - + ~
04. What is the difference between a "username" and a "display name"?
Your username is the unique name that the UltraViolet service will use to identify you. It must be unique in the UltraViolet service, and it has formatting requirements (See: "Are there guidelines for creating my username?"). Your display name on the other hand is a friendly nickname that you can use to label your profile and all of your activities when you and the other members of your account use the UltraViolet service. Display names do not have to be unique. For example, there can only be one person in the entire UltraViolet service with the username "Robert". Other users can choose names like "Robert1" or "Robertj20." But all three of the users in this example could use the display name "Robert" within their own UltraViolet Accounts.
