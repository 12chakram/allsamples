Note: some retailers may support content downloads to older devices that are not compatible with UltraViolet. Check with the retailer for your older device to determine compatibility. If the device is compatible, the retailer should have a means to register that device with UltraViolet via their own website.

To add a new UltraViolet Device to your Household, follow these easy steps:

  1. Generate an authorization token for your Device using the form below. Once generated, the authorization token will be valid for 30 minutes. Device registration must be completed within this time period or the process will have to be repeated.
  2. On your UltraViolet Device, enter the authorization token via the Device's user interface.
  3. Submit the token to enable your Device to register with the UltraViolet system.
  4. Within minutes, your Device should be registered and ready to use! Confirmation of successful Device registration is available from the Our Devices page when your new Device appears in the display of Household Devices.
